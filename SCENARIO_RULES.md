# Scenario Timing Rules (Escrow Cash Flow)

## Construction Cost Distribution (same for both scenarios)
- Month 1: 10% advance payment
- Month 2: 4%
- Month 3: 7%
- Month 4: 9%
- Month 5 onwards: 60% S-Curve
- Post month 2: 5% completion
- Post month 12: 5% retention
- Total = 100%

## Who Pays (the difference between scenarios)

### Scenario 1 (Deposit 20%)
- Investor pays: 10% advance (construction month 1) only
- Investor deposits: 20% in escrow (penultimate design month)
- Escrow pays: 90% (from deposit + sales revenue)

### Scenario 2 (No Deposit)
- Investor pays: 10% advance (construction month 1) + 20% to contractor (months 2-3-4 at 4%+7%+9%)
- No escrow deposit
- Escrow pays: 60% (from sales revenue only)
